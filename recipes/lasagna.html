Lasagna

Description

Have you ever wanted to eat a piece of heaven? well, search no more as we will give you the piece of heaven you were looking for

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone Cheese: 100g
  • Eggs: 1
  • Tomato Sauce: 200g
  • Milk: 100ml
  • Eggpalnts: 2 medium size

Steps

  1. Cut the Eggpalnts and roast them until brown
  2. Whisk the eggs for 5 minutes
  3. Start assembling the Ingredients into a sqaure over-suitable bowl
  4. Put in oven under 350 degrees for 2 hours
  5. Enjoy
